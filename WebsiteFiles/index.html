About Me

About Me

-

Outside of work I am a competitive cyclist. I've raced my bike all over the country and competed in some of the largest Pro-Am bike races, including the Joe Martin Stage Race, Tour of America's Dairyland, and The Gateway Cup.

Some fun facts about my cycling...
  • I train at least 10 hours a week, usually closer to 15.
  • The fastest I've ever gone on a bicycle is around 60 mph.
  • Since 2013, I've ridden over 50,000 miles.
  • In 2018, I rode just under 13,000 miles.
  • The longest ride I've ever completed was 402 miles , it took 26 hours 12 minutes.
  • I was the first person to everest in the state of Indiana.
  • I was also the second person to everest in Indiana, along with 3 others. Check out the hall of fame.
  • I run a YouTube Channel where I document a lot of my cycling adventures.
